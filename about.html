Lynn Suh

Editor-in-Chief / Redaktor naczelny
Editor, American Poetry

Lynn originally hails from Boston, Massachusetts, but currently resides in Krakow, Poland, working as an editor for the Polish Book Institute (Instytut Książki) and as an English literature teacher at a secondary school (PALO VIII). He is also active in the literary scene in Krakow, hosting discussions and other events in Massolit Books. As an avid reader of contemporary Polish writers, he wishes to build further bridges between Polish and American poetry through dialogue and print. He holds degrees from the U.C. Berkeley and the University of Chicago.
